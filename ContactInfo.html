All About Me

Hi! My name is Mrs. Coyle. I am an aspiring School Librarian. I have been an elementary reading teacher for 10 years. As much as I love teaching children how to read, and how to use reading to learn, my favorite thing about teaching is helping students find their joy for reading. Reading to my students, and watching them purely enjoy the stories from the words all perfectly organized within the pages, is my favorite thing. I look forward to becoming a librarian someday soon!
